What To Do If Your Pet Is Missing

  1. Search your immediate block first — check under porches, in bushes, and inside garages.
  2. Post your pet on this site so neighbors can see the details right away.
  3. Visit or call your nearest animal shelter within 24 hours and give them a photo.

How to Help a Neighbor's Lost Pet

  • Do not chase the animal — crouch down and let it approach you so it does not run further away.
  • Take a clear photo and use the Report a Sighting form to alert the owner immediately.
  • Share the lost pet post with anyone who walks or drives through the neighborhood regularly.
